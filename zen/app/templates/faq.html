    FAQ

    How does vote-reward work ?

    Voting for a delegate helps to push it within the top {{ info.delegate }} to be allowed to forge blocks. Then, its associated node can puts transactions in a block, signs it and inserts it into the blockchain. For each forged block, the delegate wallet receives {{ _currency(2, "d") }} plus the fees of forged transaction(s).

    The one runing this delegate may decide to share his reward to his voters. The most common way is to distribute rewards according to individual vote weight. With {%- for username in delegates %}{%- if loop.first %}{%- elif loop.last %} or {%- else %}, {%- endif %} {{ username }}{%- endfor %} , the share is computed and distributed according to vote weight each time a block is forged. It is the True Block Weight mechanism.

    Important thing to notify here is that your vote weight might fluctuate between two forged block according to total vote variation.

    How much may I get ?

    Since {{ info.network }} network is running, the average delay between two blocks is {{ "%.3f" % info.blocktime }} seconds. A delegate forges a block every {{ "%.3f" % (info.blocktime*info.delegate) }} seconds (average blocktime x delegate number) that makes {{ _currency(tph) }} per hour. So on a daily basis, the maximum a delegate could distribute is {{ _currency(tph*24) }}.
